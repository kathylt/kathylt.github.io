    Design Research: Safescription

    The problem space the design solution was centered around was the opioid crisis, specifically with the misuse of prescription opioids. Safescription is a pill bottle with locking capabilities, an alarm system, and programmed dosage amounts aimed to establish healthy medication adherence within the patient as well as provide security to the medication so the opioids do not end up in third-party hands.

    Case Studies and Solutions: MySeattle.gov

    The City of Seattle came to our class wanting to implement a system that makes the decision-making process of the city council more transparent and easily accessible online. MySeattle.gov is our two-pronged solution to enhance and customize user experiences and content as well as improve upon the experience of content management employees through a new content management system. Users will be able to create accounts and view content on the website based on their level of civic fluency and interests.
